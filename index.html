All about me

I'm an efficiency-minded front-end developer with a passion for pixel-perfect code high-quality code. I'm highly motivated to design and high-quality products, especially when they are coded with great efficiency and take pride in honing my skills and keeping up with the recent design and development trends.


This all started when I was 17 and designed and coded a Flash game for a School Project from scratch, while at the start my Flash abilities were somewhat limited I self-taught my way into making a something that was much bigger than I had ever done before and got an A. I I was hooked..

During my time at Bangor University I volunteered to create a website the Gaming Society there, they pushed for it to be in WordPress, this again forced me to self-teach something from scratch and in the end was able to hand over a polished product.

After University I Interned as a front end developer at a web design starts up that focused on making post-development as streamlined as possible. I learnt how to use Bootstrap framework while I was there and significantly improved my PHP. I learnt about coding practices in web development how the phases of a large project proceed.

Thanks to this I have proven experience in working on this kind of projects as a team and probably experience in HTML, CSS, javascript, as well as converting basic HTML websites into WordPress. I possess the enthusiasm and more importantly, the commitment to learn and hone my skills and develop well-organised code in a growing business, and create something I am personally proud of.
